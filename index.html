About Me

I am currently a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I have worked at the Canadian Broadcasting Cooperation as a Mobile Application Developer and at Kik Interactive as a Mobile QA Specialist for my past two Coop terms. My passions lie in Mobile Development; code examples can be found on my Github Page. I am also very passionate about Robotics and tinkering with Hardware projects. I am currently a mentor for a FIRST Robotics Team at St Davids in Waterloo.
