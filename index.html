About Me

I am currently a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. My passions lie in Mobile Development; code examples of which can be found on GitHub. I am also very passionate about Robotics and I am currently a Mentor for a FIRST Robotics Team at St. Davids high school in Waterloo, ON.

I have previously worked at CBC as a Mobile Application Developer during which I created a news application for iOS 7 from the ground up. I also have experience as a Mobile QA Specialist at Kik Interactive.

Besides technology, I like listening to music and cook during my free time.

Projects

Remembr

A simple iOS application made using the iOS 7 SDK for iPhones and iPods. The application enables the user to create catagories with custom icons and background colours to organize their notes.

CBC News

Developed a prototype CBC News Application during my coop term. The application uses the CBC backend, iOS 7 SDK, MWPhotoBrowser and a self developed HTML parser to dynamically format and layout story pages.

Kik Tech News

A simple web app created using the Kik Cards platform that downloads the feeds from two technology website, The Verge and Engadget and you can read the stories and "Kik" them to your friends.

Kik Your Memes

A simple web app created using the Kik Cards platform that enables you to pick an image from your camera or photo-gallery and add captions to them to create memes to "Kik" to your friends.
